A brief history of Mercedes-Benz

The patent motor car was developed by Carl Benz in the late 1800s and was the first of it's kind,
Many did not believe his new motor car would succeed.
Upon exiting the company one of Benz's investors told him "I'd forget about the motor car if I were you"
But Carl Benz continued his works creating motor cars that gradually spread across germany.
In the early 1920's after the great war and economic troubles DMG and Benz& Cie.
The two oldest motor car producers in the world merged to form the now known Daimler AG,
and their symbol was a merging of the mercedes star of Daimler and the laurel wreath of Benz.
